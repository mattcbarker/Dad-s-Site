ABOUT:

I love to draw and paint...I always have. I also love science and technology. I am particularly fascinated by the intersection of art and human anatomy. I am inspired by Italian Renaissance masters whose draftsmanship and skills at creating emotionally compelling portraits and figures have stood the test of time.

I draw and paint people as often as I can and I use traditional materials, paints, chalks and papers. I choose to portray the beauty, authenticity and sometimes vulnerablity I see in faces and figures... and in the fleeting moments in our everyday life.

For me, creating work like this is a form of meditation and introspection. It is personally fufilling to me and I hope this comes through in my work.

That's it...just capturing moments of beauty I see in faces and figures...it's that simple and not so deep.
